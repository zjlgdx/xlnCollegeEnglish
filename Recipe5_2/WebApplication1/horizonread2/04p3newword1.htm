acquaintance
n. 相识； 熟人
  e.g. She's an old acquaintance of mine.

  

be hooked on
be very keen on and absorbed in 对… 入了迷
  e.g. Many of the leaders have become hooked on power and money.

 

call forth
bring into action or existence 使产生，引出
  e.g. His speech called forth an angry response.

 

  

communication
n. 交流，交际，通信
  e.g. There was a lack of communications between us.
 

 

individual
n. a single human being 个人
  e.g. We designed this software in order to meet the needs of individual users.

 

  

innocent
a. knowing no evil, naive天真无邪的
n. an innocent person
  e.g. Jack is still as innocent as a child and is easily taken in.

 

intense
a. serious and concentrated 认真的，紧张的
  e.g. I have never seen an intense student like him.

 

  

needless to say
as you already know or would expect 不用说
  e.g. Needless to say, I survived the earthquake.

 

on the whole
all things considered, in general 总的看来，大体上，基本上
  e.g. On the whole, your opinion is right.

 

  

on top of
in addition to 除…之外（还）
  e.g. An extra 700 jobs are being cut on top of the 2,000 that were lost last year.

 

outwards
ad. 向外
  e.g. The door opens outwards.

 

  

overnight
ad. during or for the night 一整夜，一夜间
  e.g. If you leave your bike here overnight, it's likely to disappear.
 

 

plot
n. a plan or an outline of the events in a play or novel 情节
  e.g. The author wove a story from three plots.

 

  

scatter
vt. throw in different directions 散布
  e.g. The farmers are scattering seeds over the fields.

 

slip
vi. 溜（走），悄悄地走
  e.g. He slipped out into the garage.

 

  

tendency
n. 倾向
  e.g. There is a tendency to use plastics in place of metals.

 

to one's knowledge
as far as one knows personally 据…所知
  e.g. To my knowledge, Jack didn't get the job offer.

 

  

vehicle
n. 传播媒介；交通工具
  e.g. Air is the vehicle of sound.

 

version
n. a particular form or variation of sth. 版本
  e.g. I've read the Chinese version of the novel.

 

  

worldwide
a., ad. 全世界的；在全世界范围内
  e.g. The comet caused a worldwide panic.
