aid
n., v. help 帮助，援助
  e.g. He went to the aid of the hurt man.

 

  

bump
v. hit or knock (sth.) with a dull-sounding blow 砰地碰撞
  e.g. I bumped into a tree in the dark.

 

dismiss
vt. put (thoughts, etc.) out of one's mind; remove (sb.) from a position 摒除，排除；解除…的职务
  e.g. Peter was dismissed form his post.

 

  

ease
v. (cause to) become less tense （使）减轻
  e.g. I gave him some medicine to ease the pain.

 

hold on to
not give up; not let go of 不放弃；抓住
  e.g. He was imprisoned for 19 years and yet held on to his belief.

 

  

horrible
a. causing a feeling of extreme fear or dislike 恐怖的
  e.g. Kathy fainted when she saw the horrible sight.

 

impatient
a. not patient 不耐烦的
  e.g. He is too impatient with his kids.

 

  

in good/excellent/poor health
健康状况良好（很好，差）
  e.g. All the kids are in good health.

 

in spite of
尽管
  e.g. In spite of all his efforts, the boy did not recover.

 

  

incline
v. （使）倾向于
  e.g. Mr. Brown inclines towards conservatism.

 

install
vt. place, fix (a piece of equipment) in position for use 安装，安置
  e.g. I will definitely have a gas heater installed in my house before this winter.

 

  

interval
n. 间隔
  e.g. Trees will be planted on both sides of the street at intervals of 10 meters.

 

miserable
a. 令人痛苦的；悲惨的，不幸的
  e.g. He lived a miserable life when he was young.

 

  

optimistic
a. 乐观的
  e.g. He was optimistic about the future of mankind.

 

penetrate
vi. force a way (into or through sth.); be fully understood穿透，刺入；被充分领悟
  e.g. Our eyes could not penetrate the darkness.

 

  

progressive
a. advancing gradually by stages over a period of time 进行性的；循序渐进的
  e.g. One prominent symptom of the disease is the progressive loss of memory.

 

shortcoming
n. weakness that a person or thing has 缺点，短处
  e.g. Despite all his shortcomings, he makes a good friend.

 

  

suffer from
患…病；苦于
  e.g. She sometimes suffers from a stomachache.

 

to a degree
to a certain extent , partly 一定程度上，部分地
  e.g. These statements are, to a degree, all correct.

 

  

trait
n. 个性，特点
  e.g. The color of one's eyes is a genetic trait.
