New Words

他面临着不可挽救的婚姻破裂的事实。
He did not appear to have any financial or marital problems.
他看起来没有任何经济或婚姻问题。

premarital
a.happening before marriage
婚前的
At that time, abortion, divorce, premarital sex, and drug trafficking were listed as serious sins.
那时，流产、离婚、婚前性行为、贩卖毒品等都被列为重罪。

ice-cream
n.[U, C] very cold sweet food made from sugar and cream
冰淇淋
The child likes ice-cream very much.
这孩子喜欢冰淇淋。
The little girl had some ice-cream after dinner.
这小姑娘饭后吃了些冰淇淋。

我最爱吃巧克力冰淇淋。
Two chocolate-flavored ice-creams, please.

collection
n.1. [C] a set of things that have been collected
收藏品，收集的东西
Her new book is a collection of short stories.
她的新书是一本短篇小说集。
Our library has a large collection of books.
我们图书馆的藏书很丰富。
Bob took his insect collection to school.
鲍勃把他收集的昆虫带到了学校。
2. [U, C] collecting or gathering
收集，收取
The photos will be ready for collection on Tuesday afternoon.
照片可在星期二下午领取。
There are three collections a day from the post box on the corner.
邮递员每天从拐角的邮筒里收取三次信件。

verify
vt.make certain of or prove the truth of (sth.)
证明，证实，核实
These figures are surprisingly high and they'll have to be verified.
这些数字高得惊人，必须予以核实。

你能证明你的理论吗？

clause
n.1. [C] a particular part of a written legal document
条款，款项
There is a clause in the contract which says the company can fire people for economic reasons.
合同中有一项条款说公司可以出于经济原因而裁减人员。
The last clause says that the payment should be made within a year.
最后一项规定一年内付款。
2. [C] a group of words containing a subject and a verb, usu. forming only part of a sentence
分句，从句
A clause can be introduced with "that " or "which".
从句可以用that或which引导。

连结，联合，结合

婚姻把他俩结合在一起。
We feel bound together by our past experiences.
我们感到过去的经历把我们连在了一起。
2. tie tightly
捆绑，捆扎
They bound an umbrella to a pole to get some shade.
他们将伞绑在柱子上以得到一些荫凉。
The prisoner was bound hand and foot.
那名囚犯被绑住了手脚。
3. force sb. to obey, esp. by a law or a promise
迫使，约束
The contract binds you to completion of the work within two years.
根据合约，你必须在两年内完成这项工作。
He was bound to secrecy by the oath he took.
他已发誓保守秘密。

acceptance
n.1. [C, U] (act of) accepting or being accepted
接受，接纳

我们发出请柬后，得到的回复是五份接受一份拒绝。

2. [C, U] favor; approval
赞同，承认
The new laws gained widespread acceptance.
新法律得到广泛的赞同。

这一想法很快得到了政界的认可。

division
n.1. [U] dividing or being divided
分开，划分

一条小溪隔开了两个农场。

3. [C] one of the parts or groups in which a whole is divided

她曾在公司的国外部工作过。

她在公司的销售部工作。

所有报纸都在标题位置刊登了他逝世的消息。
I turned on the radio to get the eight o'clock headlines.

2. (take part in and) try to win (sth.)

只有两个人争夺这个位置，因此她大有希望获胜。
The firm is too small to contest with large companies.
这家商行太小了，竞争不过大公司。
n.1. [C] a competition, usu. in which prizes are given
竞赛，比赛

她在许多选美大赛中获胜。

2. [C] a struggle or fight to gain control or advantage

目前，人们正在竞争该公司的一个高层管理职务。
The contest for leadership of the party is gathering speed.
对党内领袖职务的争夺正在加剧。

candidate
n.[C] a person who is competing to get a job or position, or sb. who is taking an exam
候选人；参加考试者
Candidates must write their names on the top page of the exam paper.
参加考试者必须在试卷的头一页写上自己的名字。

60%的考生没通过考试。

他被激怒，做出了愤怒的回答。
His words certainly stung her.
毫无疑问，他的话刺伤了她。
2. wound with a sharp pain
刺伤，蛰伤
A bee stung him on the arm.
一只蜜蜂蛰了他的手臂。
n.[C] a sharp pain
刺痛，刺伤

蜘蛛跳开时，他感到肘部有刺痛。
