New Words

romantic
a.1. exciting, and having a strong effect on the emotions
传奇性的，有浪漫色彩的
We thought that Egypt was a romantic country.
我们认为埃及是一个传奇国家。
Do you like romantic love stories?
你喜欢浪漫的爱情故事吗？
2. (person) not practical and having a lot of ideas, which are not related to real life
不切实际的，空想的
She has a romantic idea of what it's like to be a struggling young artist.
她对奋斗中的年轻艺术家的形象有一种不切实际的想法。
She has a dreamy romantic nature.
她是个爱做梦的空想家。

n.1. [C] a person who works secretly for the government or other organizations
政府或其他组织的特工人员
an FBI agent
美国)联邦调查局探员
an enemy agent
敌方特务
2. [C] a person who acts for, or manages the affairs of other people in business, politics, etc.
代理人
Please contact our agent in Spain for further information.
请与我们在西班牙的代理人联系，以便得到更多信息。
He is an agent in Shanghai for a Japanese company.
他是一家日本公司驻上海的代理商。

corridor
n.[C] a long passage in a building or train, esp. with rooms on either side
走廊，通道
Her office is at the end of the corridor on the right.
她的办公室在走廊尽头靠右边。

accent
n.1. [C] the way in which people in a particular area, country, social class, etc. speak
口音，腔调
Although French is her native language, she speaks with an English accent.
尽管法语是她的母语，但她说话却带有英国口音。
I tried to get rid of my London accent when I went to university.
上大学时，我试图改变自己的伦敦腔。
2. [C] the importance given to a word or part of a word (vowel or syllable) by saying it with more force
重音，强音
The accent falls on the final word.
重音落在最后一个单词上。
In the word "today" the accent is on the second syllable.
today 一词的重音在第二个音节上。

writer
n.[C] a person who writes books or articles to be published
作家，作者
Dickens was a famous English writer.
狄更斯是位著名的英国作家。
She's a well-known writer of children's books.

mysterious
a.hard to explain or understand

作为演员，尽管她已被多次采访，她的生活仍是神秘莫测。
He died in mysterious situations, and it is possible that it was murder.
他死得很神秘，有可能是被谋杀的。

crack
n.1. [C] a sudden sharp noise
爆炸声，劈啪声

步枪的射击声打破了乡村的寂静。
He heard the sharp crack of the tree.
他听到树清脆的断裂声。
2. [C] a line along which sth. has broken, but not into separate parts
裂缝，裂纹
Don't go skating today - there are dangerous cracks in the ice.
今天不要去滑冰，冰上有危险的裂缝。
She opened the door just a crack to listen to the talking.
她把门开了一条缝听人谈话。
v.1. (cause to) do sth. with a sudden sharp noise
（使）劈啪作响，（使）发爆裂声
We heard fireworks crack all over the village.
我们听到烟火的劈劈啪啪声响遍了整个村庄。
The tree cracked loudly and fell.
那棵树发出断裂的巨响倒了下来。
2. break (sth.) open, esp. in order to reach or use what is inside
打开，砸开
He cracked three eggs into a bowl and mixed them together.
他打了三个蛋在碗里，并把它们搅在一起。
The boiling water cracked the glass.
沸水把玻璃杯烫裂了。

disguise
n.[C, U] sth. worn or used to give a false appearance
伪装，伪装物

vt.1. use, work or develop fully (esp. mines and other natural resources)
利用，开发

我们应该确使我们尽可能充分地利用资源。
You must exploit every opportunity to learn new things.
你必须利用一切机会学习新事物。
2. use unfairly for one's own advantage

许多儿童在工厂里作为廉价劳动力受到剥削。
The company exploited its workers with long hours and low pay.
该公司用长工时低工资的方法来剥削工人。

n.1. [C] a small device, usu. Pushed up or down with finger, that controls and turns on or off an electric current

Language Points

Language Point 1
Albert did not fit the description of any secret agent Charles had ever read about. (Para. 1)
Meaning: Albert was different from any secret agent that Charles had ever learned about from novels.

Language Point 2
… the description of any secret agent… (Para.1)
agent: n. a person who works for a country's secret service 
All these years he's been an agent for the Middle East. 这些年他一直是驻中东的特工。 
He was convinced he was surrounded by secret agents out to murder him. 他相信自己被追杀他的秘密特务包围了。

Language Point 3
… Charles had ever read about. (Para.1)
read about/of: receive information concerning sth. through printed material 
I know it's true, because I read about it in the official report. 我知道这是真的，因为我在官方报导中读到过。 
This important technical innovation has been read about and discussed everywhere. 关于这一重大技术革新的报导到处被人阅读和讨论。

Language Point 4
