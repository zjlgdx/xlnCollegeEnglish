New Words

perception
n.1. [U, C] awareness of things through the physical senses, esp. sight
感知，感觉，知觉
Drugs can change your perception of reality.
毒品能够改变你对现实的感知。
His perception of the change came all of a sudden.
他突然察觉到这个变化。
2. [C] a particular way of looking at or understanding sth.; an opinion
认识，观点，看法
We have to change the public's perception that money is being wasted.
我们必须改变人们认为浪费了钱的观点。
Those paintings represent the artist's perceptions and experiences.
那些绘画作品反映了艺术家的观念和经历。

accordance
n.[U] agreement; conformity
一致，和谐
We should act in accordance with the law.
我们应依法办事。
In accordance with her wishes, she was buried in France.
根据她的遗愿，她被埋葬在法国。

duration
n.[U] the length of time that sth. lasts
持续，持续期间
He planned a stay of two years' duration.
他计划呆两年。
The storm was of short duration.
暴风雨持续时间很短。

rotate
v.1. (cause to) move in circles round a central point
（使）旋转，（使）转动
The satellite slowly rotates as it circles the Earth.
卫星在绕地球旋转的同时缓慢地自转。
Rotate the handle by 180o to open the door.
将把手旋转180度打开门。
2. (cause to) take turns in a particular order
（使）轮流
The post of chairman rotates among members of committee.
主席一职由委员会成员轮流担任。
The technique of rotating crops has been introduced to the farmers.
农作物轮作技术已经介绍给农民们了。

circular
a.shaped like a circle; round
圆形的，圆的
a circular walk
环形人行道

满月呈圆形。

hydrogen
n.[U] the lightest gas with no color, taste or smell
氢

氢是一种易燃气体。
This device is used to produce hydrogen.
这个装置是用来制取氢气的。
Hydrogen and oxygen form water.
氢与氧可构成水。

nitrogen
n.[U] gas without color, taste, or smell, which forms about four-fifths of the Earth's atmosphere
氮
Nitrogen forms about 80% of the air around the Earth.
围绕地球的大气80%是氮气。

sphere
n.1. [C] an object shaped like a round ball
球形，球体
Every point on the surface of a sphere is at an equal distance from its center.
球面上的每一点到球心的距离都是相等的。
Doctors have replaced the top of his hip bone with a metal sphere.
医生用一个金属球体置换了他的股骨头。
2. [C] a range or area of activity
范围，领域
Most of our friends come from the same social sphere as we do.
我们的朋友大多和我们来自同一社会阶层。
It is sometimes difficult for film stars to keep the public and private spheres of their lives separate.
对影星来说，把公开行为和私生活分开有时是很难做到的。

primitive
a.1. of or typical of a very early stage in the development of sth.
原始的，早期的
Primitive races colonized these islands 2,000 years ago.
原始人2,000年前开垦了这些岛屿。
Even today the villagers live in a primitive one-crop economy.
直到今天，村民们仍过着原始的单一作物经济的生活。
2. simple and not sophisticated
简陋的，粗糙的
They built a primitive shelter out of tree trunks.
他们用树干建造了一个极简单的棚子。
People are living in tents, without running water and in primitive conditions.
人们住在帐篷里，没有自来水，生活条件简陋。

2. [C] a metal container filled with explosives that is fired by a large gun

a tear shell

他们向敌人开炮。

文化和社会发展现在变得极为迅速。

a.done with the hands

n.[C] a book which gives practical instructions on how to do sth. or how to use sth.

她买了一本家庭维修手册。
