  e.g. A bird had snatched up the snake from the ground and then dropped it on to the wires.

n.,v. 用力拉；拖拽

  e.g. She swung around, almost losing her balance.

 

take off
突然离去，溜走；（飞机）起飞
  e.g. The plane eventually took off at 11 o'clock.

 

  

(at) full blast
with maximum power, energy or noise 全速地；大力地；极响地
  e.g. Do you really need the heater on at full blast?

 

stoop

  e.g. He stooped to look under the table.

  e.g. His frail hands could hardly hold a cup.

  e.g. As soon as I get through with my work I'll join you.

 

pop out

furnish

switch on
