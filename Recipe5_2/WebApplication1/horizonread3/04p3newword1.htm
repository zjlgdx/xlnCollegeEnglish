take ... for granted
assume that (sth.) is true without questioning it; benefit from without showing appreciation 认为……理所当然
  e.g. He seemed to take it for granted that he should speak as a representative.

 

be true of
be valid or relevant for 对……适用
  e.g. Expenditure on health in most of these countries has gone down, and the same is true of education.

 

in theory
理论上
  e.g. The plans are good in theory, but they won't work in practice.

 

  

detect
vt. discover 察觉，发觉
  e.g. The dentist could detect no sign of decay in her teeth.

 

in particular
especially 尤其
  e.g. He stressed that point in particular.

 

  

in connection with
with regard to 有关；关于
  e.g. Several men are detained in connection with the robbery.

 

reliable
a. dependable 可靠的，可信赖的
  e.g. It's not reliable to judge a man only by his looks.

 

   for one thing
(used to introduce one of two or more possible reasons for sth.) 首先一点，一来
  e.g. For one thing, people now enjoy a higher standard of living.

 

stable
a. fixed or unchanging 稳定的
  e.g. Markets are flourishing and prices are stable.

 

  

orbit
n. 轨道
v. 绕……轨道而行
  e.g. Most planetary orbits are not circles but ellipsis.

 

for instance
for example 例如
  e.g. I've lived in many cities, for instance, Boston, Chicago and Detroit.

 

  

be apt to (do)
be likely to (do) 有……倾向，易于
  e.g. It's apt to be cold in the evenings.  

 

evolution
n. a gradual process of developing 演变，进展，发展
  e.g. The space program is the evolution of years of research.  

 

 

in other words
that is to say 换言之，换句话说
  e.g. In other words, when the wind is blowing at 120 kilometers per hour, most waves will be about twelve meters.

 

existence
n. the fact or state of existing 存在
  e.g. The elephant is the largest land animal in existence.

 

   capable
a. having the ability required for a specific task 有能力的
  e.g. She is proud of her son, who's very capable as a driver.

 

endure
v. carry on through; last; suffer patiently 持久；耐久；忍耐
  e.g. His fame will endure for ever.

 

  

dispose of
deal with conclusively; get rid of, throw away 处置，解决；除掉；扔掉
dispose v. settle or regulate 处理，处置
  e.g. We have to dispose of that pile of old papers and magazines.

 

in the process (of)
在……的过程中
  e.g. The firm is now in the process of moving the main equipment to a new place.

 

  

universe
n. 宇宙，世界
  e.g. Our world is but a small part of the universe.
