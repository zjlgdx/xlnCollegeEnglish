outline
vt. give a short general description of (sth). 概括；概述
  e.g. We outlined our main objections to the proposal.

 

at the start of
在…开始时
  e.g. We are only at the start of our job-hunting.

 

implication
n. a thing that is suggested or implied; a thing that is not openly stated 含意；暗示
  e.g. He smiled, with the implication that he didn't believe me.

 

  

thereby
ad. by that means or action 因此，由此，从而
  e.g. He became a citizen, thereby gaining the right to vote.

 

feasible
a. that can be done; possible 可行的；可能的；有理的
  e.g. It is not feasible to follow your proposals.

 

  

realistic
a. having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be done, achieved, etc. 现实的，实际的
  e.g. She gave us a realistic appraisal of our chances.

 

in cooperation with
与…合作，与…协同
  e.g. The report was produced by the Government in cooperation with the chemical industry.

 

   en route
ad., a. (French) on the way; while traveling 在途中（的）
  e.g. We are en route to the museum.

 

component
n. any of the parts of which sth. is made 元件，组件；部件，零件
  e.g. Blade and handle are the component parts of a knife.

 

  

diverse
a. of different kinds; varied 多种多样的；形形色色的
  e.g. Her interests are very diverse.

 

permanent
a. lasting or expected to last for a long time or for ever 永久的；持久的
  e.g. She is looking for permanent employment.

 

  

implement
vt. put (sth.) into effect; carry (sth.) out 贯彻；执行；实施
  e.g. The committee's decision will be implemented immediately.  

 

equivalent
n. a thing, amount, word, etc. that is equal in value, importance, etc. 相等物；等价物
  e.g. Is there a French word that is the exact equivalent of the English word "home"?  

 

 

appropriate
a. suitable, acceptable or correct in the circumstances 适当的，恰当的，相称的
  e.g. His casual clothes were not appropriate for such a formal occasion.

 

by means of
by using (sth.); with the help of (sth.) 用，依靠
  e.g. We crossed the stream by means of a log.

 

   assembly
n. the action or process of fitting together the parts of sth. 装配，组装   e.g. About 50 people are working on this assembly line.

 

exposure
n. 暴露，显露
  e.g. He nearly died of exposure on the cold mountain.

 

  

mature
v. become or make (sb./sth.) mature （使）成熟，（使）长成
  e.g. The child's judgment matures as she grows older.

 

introduction
n. the bringing of (sth.) into use or operation for the first time 采用；引进
  e.g. The introduction of new advanced techniques in the factory is under discussion today.

 

  

perceive
vt. interpret or understand (sth.) in a certain way 把…看作，认为
  e.g. I perceived his comment as a challenge.
