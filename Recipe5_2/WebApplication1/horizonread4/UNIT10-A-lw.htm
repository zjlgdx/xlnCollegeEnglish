New Words

EQ
n.(abbr.) emotional quotient, using emotion as a measure of human intelligence
情商
Instead the article attacked what I had said or written about EQ in other places.
相反，这篇文章对我在其他地方所说的和所写的有关情商一些话进行了攻击。
My condition for accepting the invitation to participate in a debate on EQ was that I be shown in advance any press release advertising the debate, for I feared being misrepresented.
我接受邀请参加有关情商辩论的条件是对这次辩论作宣传的任何新闻稿要事先给我看，因为我担心遭到歪曲。

IQ
n.(abbr.) intelligence quotient, a measure of human intelligence
智商
Children with higher blood lead levels did less well in IQ tests, number skills and reading ability.
血液中铅含量较高的儿童在智商测试、数字技能和阅读能力等方面表现欠佳。
He discovered that the girl had an IQ of genius level and announced that she needed firm discipline and intellectual stimulation.
他发现那女孩拥有天才水准的智商，并声称她需要严格训练和智力方面的激励。

incorporate
vt.make sth. a part of sth. larger; include
把……合并，使并入
This is largely because, once incorporated into the academy, this study had to adopt the values and principles of the academy.
这在很大程度上是因为这项研究一旦并入到这所研究院，就得采纳研究院的价值观和原则。
These exercises can easily be incorporated into a routine exercise, with each exercise repeated a number of times.
这些操练能很容易地并入一种日常例行的锻炼之中，每种操练重复几遍。

maturity
n.[U] the state or time of being mature
成熟情况; 成熟时期
Many fruits change color as they reach maturity and this can be a useful indicator.
许多水果成熟时颜色会变，这是一种很有用的标示。
When he reached maturity, we would be in a position to know precisely which of his behavioral patterns he had inherited and which he had learned.
他发育成熟时，我们就能确切知道他的哪些行为模式是遗传的，哪些是学会的。

trigger
vt.start or cause sth. undesirable to happen
触发，引起
The two gunmen triggered a massive search when they fled in a stolen car and opened fire.
这两个枪手驾驶着一辆偷来的车逃走，并开了枪，触发了一场大范围的搜查。
His heart attack was triggered by the physical and emotional pressures suffered under interrogation.
他的心脏病发作是讯问时在身体和感情上所受的压力引起的。

make a contribution to
do sth. to help to cause sth.
对……作出贡献
Everyone in the area had made a contribution to the family restaurant market, which enabled people to have a very good value meal in enjoyable surroundings, for less money.
这个地区的每个人都对家庭式餐馆市场作出了贡献。这市场使得人们能在愉快的环境中享受到真正物有所值的膳食，而花的钱不多。
Every one of you has made a contribution to the character of this institution and I take pleasure in acknowledging what each of you has done to make this a satisfying academic and social environment.
你们每个人都对这所学校的特色做出了贡献，我很高兴地感谢你们每个人所做的一切，使其成为一个令人满意的学术和社会环境。

sort through
look for sth. among a lot of similar things, esp. when one arranges these things in order
整理，捡出
They sorted through the books and divided them into two lots.
他们整理了书籍，把它们分成两份儿。
I have been sorting through these old papers to see what can be thrown away.
我在整理这些旧文件，看看哪些可以扔掉。

aim at
intend or plan to do or achieve sth.
旨在，追求
When he was in university, he was aiming at a scholarship.
上大学时，他的目标是要获得一个奖学金。
But that is the effect, he wrote, that is the effect I am aiming at.
但那就是结果，他写道，那是我追求的结果。

on purpose
intentionally, not accidentally
故意，特意
Whether on purpose or by mistake, Andrew was exchanged for the baby who came to be known as Steve.
不管是故意的还是由于差错，安德鲁换成了后来叫史蒂夫的那个男孩。
I was always afraid that Hindley would hurt his small son, either by accident or on purpose, when he was drunk.
我总担心欣德利喝醉时，他会有意无意地伤害他的小儿子。

in the act of doing (sth.)
while sb. is doing (sth.)
正在做某事时
He turned and, in the act of rising, felt his hat being swept off his head.
他转过身，正要站起时，感到他的帽子从头上被吹走了。
Any one of us may arrest a person who is in the act of committing a crime.
我们中任何人都可以逮捕一个正在实施犯罪的人。

take note of
pay attention to
注意，注意到
The government has taken note of objections.
该政府已经注意到反对意见。
But the names mean nothing to me because I only take note of the roles actors take in the play.
可是名字对我来说无所谓的，因为我只注意演员们在戏中的角色。

set aside
save or keep money or time for a particular purpose
拨出，留下以备将来使用
Thus if you set aside 500 dollars per month, it would take two years to accumulate the minimum sum needed for your kid.
这样，如果你每月留出500美元，要两年才能攒到你小孩所需要的那笔钱的最低数量。
A special period of at least two weeks should be set aside solely for the purpose of carrying out an inspection.
至少两周的时间要专门留出来完全用于执行检查的目的。

reflect on/upon
consider (sth.) carefully and at length
思考，考虑，沉思
He reflected on the options which had been open to him, one of which had led to his downfall.
他反省那些自己以前一直可有的选择，其中有一选择导致了他的垮台。
After reflecting on the problem, he decided not to go there.
他仔细考虑了这个问题后决定不去。

pull away from
cause (sb. or sth.) to leave (sth. or sb.)
使脱身，使脱离
Harvey elbowed his way through and, grasping Lisa by the hand, pulled her away from David, and escorted her out of the room.
哈维挤过人群，抓住利萨的手，把她从大卫那儿拉开，并护送她走出了房间。
As if an unknown force were compelling her, Katherine strode towards the woman, pulled her away from her partner.
就好像有一股无名的力量迫使着她，凯瑟琳大步向那妇女走去，把她从她同伴中拉开。

keep at
continue working at (sth.)
继续做，坚持干
I keep at it — whatever it is — and don't give up too easily.
不管它是什么，我都会坚持干，不会轻易放弃。
We know it's easy to say, but you'll soon start to see and feel the benefits of dieting, providing you keep at it.
虽然我们知道说起来容易，但是只要你坚持，你不久就会看到并体会到节食的益处。

Daniel Goleman
丹尼尔·戈尔曼（人名）

Language Points

Language Point 1
What is the most valuable contribution employees make to their companies, knowledge or judgment? (Para. 1)
Meaning: What is the most important thing employees do to make their companies successful? Is it knowledge or judgment?

Language Point 2
What is the most valuable contribution employees make to their companies, knowledge or judgment? (Para. 1)
make a contribution to: do sth. to help to cause sth.   
This would, we believe, have the effect of facilitating the professional response to such work and make a contribution to the management of worker stress. 我们相信这会起到促进对这种工作做出职业反应的作用，并有助于控制工人所承受的压力。   
He made an important contribution to the company’s success.他对公司的成功做出了重要的贡献。

Language Point 3
Knowledge, no matter how broad, is useless until it is applied. (Para. 1)
Meaning: Knowledge is useful only when it is put to use, however broad it is.

Language Point 4
And application takes judgment, which involves something of a sixth sense — a high performance of the mind. (Para. 1)
Meaning: To apply knowledge requires the ability to form insightful opinions and make right decisions, which involves, to some degree, knowing things instinctively or intuitively without any direct evidence of them — using the mind efficiently.

Language Point 5
…which involves something of a sixth sense — a high performance of the mind. (Para. 1)
a sixth sense: an ability to know sth. without using the five senses of sight, hearing, smell, touch and taste   
I had a sixth sense that the train was going to crash.我有一种直觉，这列火车将发生车祸。   
Somehow, I'm sure that wild animals have a sixth sense that tells them they're safe, even in the presence of their enemies.不知何故，我肯定野生动物具有一种直觉能告诉它们处境安全，即使是有敌人出现的时候。   
A sixth sense warned her that he was deliberately trying to infuriate her, to make her angry enough to lose control. 一种直觉告诫她：他是在故意想要激怒她，使得她愤怒至极而失去控制。

Language Point 6
…indicate that intelligent but inflexible people don't have the right stuff in an age when the adaptive ability is the key to survival. (Para. 2)
Meaning: …show that people who are intelligent but not flexible to respond to changes don't possess the right ability in an age when the adaptive ability is important to survival.

Language Point 7
…when the adaptive ability is the key to survival. (Para. 2)
Note that after the word “key”, a preposition “to” is used. Some nouns are usually followed by a prepositional phrase which consists of to and a noun group:   
answer to the question 问题的答案
access to all information 使用全部资料的权利
admission to the university 进入这所大学
a new approach to language teaching 语言教学的新方法
damage to the environment 对环境的破坏
solution to the problem 这个问题的解答
preface to the book 书的前言

Language Point 8
In a recent cover story, Time magazine sorted through the current thinking on intelligence…(Para. 3)
a cover story: a story or an article with the illustrative picture on the front page of a magazine

Language Point 9
…Time magazine sorted through the current thinking on intelligence…(Para. 3)
sort through: look for sth. among a lot of similar things, esp. when one arranges these things in order   
She found the ring while sorting through some clothes. 她在整理衣服的时候发现了戒指。   
The directors were working overtime in the boardroom, sorting through thirty applications for the manager's job; they've drawn up a shortlist of four men who are to be interviewed over the next couple of days. 这些董事们正在会议室加班，整理三十份申请这一经理职位的申请书。他们已经拟出一份四人名单，在今后两三天内将对这四个人进行面试。

Language Point 10
If the evolutionary pressures of the marketplace are making EQ, not IQ, the hot ticket for business success, it seems likely that individuals will want to know how to cultivate it. (Para. 4)
Meaning: With the development of business, the pressures from the market are becoming higher and higher and this causes EQ, rather than IQ, to be the key to business success. In this case, individuals are very likely to want to know how to develop and improve EQ.

Language Point 11
If the evolutionary pressures of the marketplace are making EQ, not IQ, the hot ticket for business success, … (Para. 4)
the hot ticket: here it refers to what is needed or suitable to be successful and everyone wants it

Language Point 12
… it seems likely that individuals will want to know how to cultivate it. (Para. 4)
cultivate: v.   
1) try to acquire or develop a relationship, an attitude, etc.   
Throughout his life he cultivated and maintained the confidence of his African colleagues, and his faithfulness and loyalty to the African cause were profound. 他毕生致力于帮助他的非洲同事们培养和保持信心，他对非洲事业忠心耿耿。   
They cultivated the virtues of hard work, celebrated the advantages of competition and the accessibility of success for any able and determined pupil, and efficiently pursued victory in the examination system.他们培养努力工作的美德，颂扬竞争的好处和任何有能力有决心的学生都能取得成功的观念，并在这考试系统中有效地追求成功。   
They are cultivating relationships with these countries. 他们正在同这些国家建立发展关系。   
2) prepare and use land, soil, etc. for growing crops; grow crops   
Most of the land there is too poor to cultivate. 那里的大部分土地太贫瘠而无法耕种。   
It is thought that rice was first cultivated in India and Indo-China, but it is now grown extensively throughout the Far East. 人们认为水稻最初是在印度和印度支那耕种，而现在在整个远东地区被广泛种植。

Language Point 13
I have a modest proposal: Embrace a highly personal practice aimed at improving these four adaptive skills. (Para. 4)
Meaning: I have a small proposal that we accept and believe in wholeheartedly a highly personal practice which is intended to improve these four adaptive skills.

Language Point 14
… Embrace a highly personal practice aimed at improving these four adaptive skills. (Para. 4)
embrace: v.   
1) accept enthusiastically   
You'd be a fool not to embrace an opportunity as good as this. 不抓住那么好的机会你真是个傻瓜。   
Most of the major West European countries have embraced the concept of high speed rail networks. 大部分西欧主要国家已满腔热忱地接受高速铁路网的概念。   
2) hold (sb.) tightly with both arms to express love, liking or sympathy, or when greeting or leaving sb.   
The mother embraced her baby tenderly. 这位母亲温柔地搂住她的孩子。   
People embraced and wept, promising to stay in touch, only to realise in some cases that they had no addresses to exchange.人们相互拥抱，流下了眼泪，答应要保持联系，结果却意识到在某种情况下他们没有地址可以交换。   
3) include   
The term “mankind” embraces men, women and children. “人类”一词包括男人、女人和儿童。
The reform plan also embraced exchange control over all foreign currencies, the cutting of subsidies to loss-making industries, high interest rates and new incentives for agricultural development. 这项改革方案也包括对所有外国货币的汇兑控制，对亏损行业补贴的削减，实行高利率和为农业发展而采取的新的奖励政策。

Language Point 15
… Embrace a highly personal practice aimed at improving these four adaptive skills. (Para. 4)
aim at:  intend or plan to do or achieve sth.   
The government's campaign is aimed at influencing public opinion. 政府开展这场运动的目的是为了影响公众舆论。   
We are aiming at a higher production level. 我们的目标是达到一个更高的生产水平。

Language Point 16
…improving these four adaptive skills. (Para. 4)

Language Point 17
Raising consciousness. (Para. 5)
consciousness: n. all the ideas, thoughts, feelings, etc. of a person or people; interest in or knowledge of a particular subject or idea   
The experience helps to change her social consciousness. 这次经历有助于改变她的社会意识。 
What remains uncertain is whether consciousness is a separate phenomenon which needs explaining, like language or vision. 仍然还不能肯定的是意识是不是像语言或幻觉一样是一种需要加以阐明的独立现象。

Language Point 35

Language Point 37

Language Point 38
…is nothing less than a form of enlarging your senses. (Para. 8)
nothing less than: the phrase is used to emphasize how great or extreme sth. is

Their dream was nothing less than a revolutionary project to bring computers and ordinary people together. 他们梦想着能把计算机和普通老百姓联系在一起，这简直就像是一项革命性的工程。

Language Point 39
…a form of enlarging your senses. (Para. 8)
enlarge: v.   
1) (cause to) become bigger; broaden   
The factory was enlarged to make room for more machinery.那家工厂扩建厂房以安装更多的机器。   
The zoom control would allow the viewer to enlarge any area of the picture displayed. 变焦控制使得取镜器能对所展现图景的任何范围进行放大。   
2) develop a bigger print of a photograph   
They had some of their wedding photos enlarged.他们把他们的一些结婚照片放大了。 
Here is a photograph twice enlarged from the original. 这是一张根据原件放大一倍的照片。   
Compare:
expand: v. increase (sth.) in size, number or importance   
The air in the balloon expands when heated.气球内的气体受热膨胀。   
They expanded their retail operations significantly during the 1980's. 在20世纪80年代，他们大大地扩充了零售业务。

Language Point 40
The next time someone interprets something differently from you — say, a controversial political event — pause to reflect on the role of life experience and consider it a gift of perception. (Para. 8)
Meaning: The next time someone has a different interpretation of something from ours, for example, a political event that causes disagreement or discussion, we should think about the role of life experience in enlarging our senses and consider his or her different interpretation as a gift to broaden our perception. The phrase “the role of life experience” implies that people have different life experiences and they look at something based on their own life experiences and, as a result, their interpretation of something may be different from ours. The phrase “a gift of perception” here refers to the fact that people's different interpretation or the way of thinking is taken as a gift for us to broaden our own perception.

Language Point 41
…say, a controversial political event…(Para. 8)
controversial: a. causing disagreement and discussion   
a controversial issue /decision /speech /policy 有争议的事件/决定/演讲/政策

These buildings are so controversial that passers-by, princes or those who work in them feel moved to express an opinion, either positive or negative. 这些建筑很有争议，连行人、王子或里面的工作人员都感到有必要表达自己或肯定或否定的看法。

Language Point 42
…pause to reflect on the role of life experience and consider it a gift of perception. (Para. 8)
reflect on/upon: consider (sth.) carefully and at length   
The manager demanded time to reflect on what to do. 经理要求给他时间考虑怎么做。   
In this paper we will argue that the involvement of disabled people in the arts is an indication of the general state of their success in reflecting upon and managing their own affairs. 在这篇论文中我们认为残疾人参与艺术表明了他们在考虑和管理自己事务方面取得成功的总体状况。

Language Point 43
…consider it a gift of perception. (Para. 8)
perception: n.   
1) one's ability to notice and understand things that are not obvious to other people, insight   
Companies rely on the perceptions of their own managers on the spot, with a loyalty to head office. 公司依赖于它们自己那些在现场的、忠于总公司的经理们的洞察力。   
The success of the project displayed his penetrating perception. 这个项目的成功显示了他敏锐的洞察力。   
2) a belief or opinion, often held by many people and based on appearances   
We have to change the public's perception that money is being wasted. 我们必须改变公众的看法。他们认为钱正在被浪费。   
These photographs will affect people's perceptions of war. 这些照片会影响人们对战争的看法。   
She thinks the microwave has changed our perceptions of time, much as telephones changed them at the turn of the century. 她认为就像在本世纪初电话曾改变了我们对时间的看法一样，微波也已改变了我们对时间的看法。

Language Point 44

Meaning: The power of habit, that is, the fixed pattern of thinking in your mind, will prevent you from practicing these skills. The word “literally” is used for emphasizing the part “the established wiring of your brain”.

Language Point 45
The force of habit — literally the established wiring of your brain — will pull you away from practicing these skills. (Para. 9)
pull away from: cause (sb. or sth.) to leave (sth. or sb.)   
Do pull yourself away from the window; there can't be anything interesting out there. 离开窗户吧，外面不可能有什么有趣的东西。

Language Point 48
… and every experience triggers a brain response that literally shapes our senses. (Para. 10)
trigger: v. cause (sth.) to start   
Some people find that certain foods trigger their headaches. 有些人发现某些食物能引起他们头痛。   
The racial killings at the weekend have triggered off a wave of protests throughout the country.周末发生的种族杀戮引发了全国性的抗议浪潮。   
The oil price and supply uncertainties triggered awareness of the problems of depending on imported energy supplies. 石油价格和供应的不稳定使人们意识到依赖进口能源供应的种种问题。

Language Point 49
The mind, we now know, is not confined to the brain but is distributed throughout the body's universe of cells. (Para. 10)
Meaning: We know now that the ability to think, feel emotions and be aware of things is not limited to the brain only but is found in every cell of the human body.

Language Point 50
The mind, we now know, is not confined to the brain…(Para. 10)
confine to: limit (sth., sb., or oneself) to (sth.)   
I don't like a job in which I'm confined to doing only one thing. 我不喜欢仅限于做一件事的工作。   
Could you confine your discussions to the matter in question? 请你们把讨论集中在正题上。

Meaning: When that is finished, what one of the nation's leading brain researchers calls “our view of the world” has been shaped /has become fixed biologically.

Language Point 52
So unique is the personal experience that people would understand the world differently. (Para. 11)
Note the inversion of the word order after so unique:   
So successful was her business that she was able to retire at the age of 50. 她的生意非常成功，因此她在50岁就可以退休了。   
So unlikely did his story sound that no one believed him. 他说的事情听起来非常不可能，没人相信他的话。   
Compare :   
Such is the popularity of the play that the theatre is likely to be full every night.
