Language Point 13
Broadly speaking, however, the superhighway refers to the union…(Para. 4)
broadly speaking: generally speaking

The refugees who were to be involved in the resettlement programs fell, broadly speaking, into two categories. 参与重新定居计划的难民大体上分为两类。
There are some expressions formed by "-ly + speaking" in English. They are used to make it clear how you are speaking (or indeed writing), for example, "broadly speaking", "generally speaking", and "roughly speaking". "Strictly speaking" means that you are claiming to be very accurate and precise. Expressions such as "technically speaking" or "medically speaking" are used to mean you are speaking from a technical or medical point of view. Often, though, "technically speaking" means much the same as "strictly speaking".
