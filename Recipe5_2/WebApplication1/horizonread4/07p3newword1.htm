crew

scout
v. go looking for (sth.) 侦察，搜索，寻找 n. 侦察员，侦察机
  e.g. We'd better start scouting for a new secretary.

 

tip off
give a piece of secret information or a warning to (sb.) 向…密报，事先警告
  e.g. Someone tipped off the police about the robbery.

  e.g. He was arrested when customs officers found drugs in his bag.

  e.g. The child is going through a difficult phase.

 

put/lay/place emphasis on

  e.g. Some schools put great emphasis on language study.

 

  

undergo
vt. (underwent undergone) experience or endure 经历，遭受，忍受
  e.g. The ship successfully underwent sea trials in coastal waters.

 

tempt
vt. persuade, attract 诱惑，吸引
  e.g. He was tempted into a life of crime by greed and laziness.

 

  

book
v. reserve (a room, seat, table etc.) 预订，登记
  e.g. Book early if you want to be sure of a seat.  

 

justice
n. 司法，审判；正义，正当，公平
  e.g. He demanded, with some justice, that he should be given an opportunity to express his views.  

 

 

pose
