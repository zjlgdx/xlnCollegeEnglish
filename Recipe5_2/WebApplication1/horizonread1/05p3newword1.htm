1. come across
meet or discover by chance 偶然遇见，碰上
  e.g. We've just come across an old friend we haven't seen for ages.

 

  

2. make out
manage to see or read 辨认出
  e.g. I could barely make out the traffic signs through the rain.

 

3. hope for
want and expect (sth.) 希望，期待
  e.g. I hope for going to college.

 

  

4. clue
n. 线索
  e.g. The police found a clue which will help them catch the robber.

 

5. identity
n. 身份
  e.g. The police are still uncertain of the murderer's identity.

 

  

6. forbid
(forbade or forbad, forbidden) vt. 禁止，不许;妨碍
  e.g. Smoking is forbidden in the concert hall.

 

7. other than
except; apart from 除了
  e.g. There's nobody here other than me.

 

  

8. exchange
vt. 交换，互换
  e.g. Let's exchange our views on the matter.

 

9. match up to
be as good as ( sb. or sth.) 与...一样好;比得上
  e.g. My new dress matches up to the one you have.

 

  

10. for sure
(infml) certainly so, without doubt 毫无疑问
  e.g. We'll win for sure.

 

11. goodness
int. 天哪
  e.g. My goodness!

 

  

12. relief
n. 轻松，宽慰
  e.g. I felt great relief when I heard I had passed the examination.

 

13. come to an end
finish 结束
  e.g. What time does the concert come to an end?

 

  

14. knot
n.（绳等的）结
  e.g. tie a knot in a piece of string

 

15. erect
a. upright, standing on end 挺直的，直立的
vt. 使直立
  e.g. This monument was erected in honor of the martyrs during the internal war.
