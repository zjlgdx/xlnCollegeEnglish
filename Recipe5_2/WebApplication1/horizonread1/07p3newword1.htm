1. wrap
vt. cover or roll up 包，裹
  e.g. The shop assistant wrapped up the dress for her.

 

  

2. trap
vt. 使陷于困境;使坠入陷阱
n. 陷阱
  e.g. The police trapped the thieves.

 

3. conceal
vt. hide 隐藏
  e.g. He concealed the sweets in his pocket.

 

  

4. pressure
n. 压力
  e.g. The pressures of modern life are great.

 

5. plunge
vi. jump or fall suddenly and with force 纵身跳进
  e.g. He plunged into the water to save the child.

 

  

6. crush
v. 压坏，压伤
  e.g. Don't crush this box; it has flowers in it.

 

7. snap
vi. (啪地一声)折断
  e.g. A branch snapped off the tree in the wind.

 

  

8. squeeze
n., v. 捏;挤压
  e.g. Don't squeeze the kitten; you will hurt it.
 

 

9. collapse
vi. 晕倒;坍塌;垮下
vt. 毁坏
  e.g. The old man collapsed in the street.

 

  

10. shiver
vi. 颤抖，哆嗦
  e.g. He shivered as he heard the strange noise in the night.

 

11. be up to (sb.)
be the duty or responsibility of 是...的职责
  e.g. It'up to us to give them all the help we can.

 

  

12. nowhere
n. no place 不存在的地方，不知名的地方
  e.g. He is nowhere to be seen.  

 

13. presence
n. 在场， 存在
  e.g. She was so quiet that her presence was hardly noticed.  

 

  

14. pray
v. 祈祷
  e.g. She prayed silently.

 

15. hang on
continue with effort 坚持
  e.g. It's hard work, but if you hang on long enough you'll succeed.
