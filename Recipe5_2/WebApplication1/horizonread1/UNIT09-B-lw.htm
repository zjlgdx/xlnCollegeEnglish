他问了我一个很难的问题。

Language Points

Language Point 1
Trends for the 21st Century (Title)
trend: n. general direction or course of development
Nowadays a trend towards part-time employment emerges. 如今出现了一个打工潮。
There is a growing trend in the United States to exercise regularly and eat wisely. 在美国出现了一个趋势：有规律地锻炼，有节制地饮食。

Language Point 2
Social scientists and economists, farming experts and environmentalists pose this question and examine data, information from surveys. (Para. 1)
Meaning: Social scientists and economists, farming specialists and environmentalists are all asking this question and looking at data, information from studies they have made.

Language Point 3
Social scientists and economists, farming experts and environmentalists pose this question ...(Para. 1)
expert: n. specialist
She is an expert in/at teaching children. 她是儿童教育专家。
We respect his skill because he is an expert at his work. 我们尊重他的技术，因为他是那个行当的专家。
pose: 1. vt.  ask
He posed a difficult question to me. 他问了我一个很难的问题。
2. vi.  stand or sit in a particular position to be drawn, photographed, etc.
She was employed by the art school to pose for student artists. 这个艺术学校雇她给学生做模特。

Language Point 4
...and examine data, information from surveys. (Para. 1)
data: n. facts; information (used in the plural)
This data are all ready for examination. 供考试使用的资料已准备好了。
The data aren't complete, and I don't think they are correct. 数据不完整，同时我也觉得这些数据不正确。
Scientists watch the moon to get data about its movement. 科学家们观察月亮，以收集其运行的数据。
survey: n. act of finding out information about something; study
a survey of public opinion 对舆论的调查
A survey shows that students today have more assignments to do. 一项调查表明，现在学生的作业量增加了。
vt. look at or consider as a whole
The buyers surveyed the goods offered for sale. 买主查看了那些摆出来供出售的商品。
The engineers were asked to survey the damage done by the flood. 这位工程师被请来察看水灾造成的损失。

Language Point 5
In every field, experts examine changes to understand the state of the field. (Para. 2)
Meaning: In every field, experts look at the changes in the field so as to have a better understanding of the field's situation and development.

Language Point 6

3. the act or rate of growing and developing
Trees takes many years to reach their full growth. 树长成材需要很多年。

Language Point 9

Language Point 11

Language Point 12

Language Point 16
As the number of women going to school increases, the growth rate declines. (Para. 5)
Meaning: The population growth rate in China becomes lower because more and more women are going to school.
decline: v. 1. become worse or less
The number of staff has declined from 12,000 to 10,000. 职工的人数从一万二千人降到了一万人。
The value of the dollar on the world market has declined in recent years. 最近几年美元在世界市场上的价值已有所下降。
The number of smokers in the United States declined greatly in the 1980s. 二十世纪80年代美国吸烟人数大量减少。
2. refuse usually politely; being unwilling
We had to decline the invitation to visit our friends in another city. 我们只得谢绝了去另一城市拜访朋友的邀请。
The football star declined to answer the news writer's question. 那位足球明星拒绝回答新闻记者的提问。
n. process of becoming worse or less
If something is in decline, falling into decline, or on the decline, it is slowly growing smaller, weaker, or worse. 如果什么东西在缩减、衰弱或衰退，那它就是在渐渐变小、变弱或变差。
Thankfully the smoking of cigarettes is on the decline. 谢天谢地，抽烟的人越来越少了。

Language Point 17
Natural disasters like storms and floods have washed away many crops. (Para. 7)
Meaning: Natural events such as storms and floods have destroyed many crops.
disaster: n. sudden serious hard times
The town has taken a long time to recover from the disaster of the flood. 这个城镇用了很长时间才从水灾的损失中恢复过来。
It would be a disaster for them not to reach the semi-finals. 他们要是打不进半决赛就太让人失望了。
wash away: destroy (something such as a road or bridge) by the action of a lot of water
The river banks have been washed away by the flood. 河岸被洪水冲毁了。

Language Point 18
With less land for cattle and sheep, less meat like beef and lamb is being produced. (Para. 8)
Meaning: As we have less land for cattle and sheep, we are producing less meat like beef and lamb.

Language Point 19
The amount of ocean fish has not increased, but fish farm production has. (Para. 8)
Meaning: Although there has been no increase in the amount of ocean fish, more fish are coming from fish farms.
fish farm production: production of fish on farms; raising fish on farms 水场养殖
amount: n. quantity, total
Large amounts of money were spent on the bridge. 建造这座桥花了许多钱。
The food bill was an unbelievable amount of money. 用餐费多得难以置信。
That amount will not be enough to serve six people. You will have to cook more. 这些食物不够6个人吃，得再做一点。
Please notice that "amount" is used with uncountable nouns. With plurals it is better to use "number". Study the following phrases:
the amount of money 钱的数额
the number of mistakes 错误的数量
"Amount" can be used as a verb, too.
v. equal to
The words in the composition amount to 500. 这篇作文的字数达到了500个。
Also notice that when "amount" is used as a verb it always takes the preposition "to".
