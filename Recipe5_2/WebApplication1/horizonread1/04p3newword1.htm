1. surroundings
n. 环境;周围的事物
  e.g. The house is situated in very pleasant surroundings.

 

  

2. threaten
vt. 威胁，恐吓
  e.g. The terrorists threatened to blow up the plane if their demands were not met.

 

3. divorce
v. legally end a marriage (to sb.) 离婚;与...离婚
  e.g. My parents divorced when I was very young.

 

  

4. motivate
vt. 激发起...的兴趣，激励
  e.g. He was motivated by love, and expected nothing in return.
 

 

5. assure
vt. cause (sb.) to be sure, to feel certain 使确信;使有信心
  e.g. He assured us of his ability to solve the problem.

 

  

6. soar
vi. rise rapidly 骤升，猛增
  e.g. The temperature soared to 80°.

 

7. hardship
n. 苦难，困苦
  e.g. Many people are suffering economic hardship.

 

  

8. fulfill
vt. cause (sth. wished for or planned) to happen; perform or complete (a task,duty,etc.) 实现;履行，完成
  e.g. A nurse has many duties to fulfill in caring for the sick.

 

9. obtain
vt. get 获得，得到
  e.g. I haven't been able to obtain that record anywhere.

 

  

10. appoint
vt. 任命，委任;约定（时间等）
  e.g. We have decided to appoint a new teacher.

 

11. sacrifice
vt., n. 牺牲;舍弃
  e.g. It is the company's policy to sacrifice short-term profits for the sake of long-term growth.

 

  

12. come up with
think of (a plan, reply, etc.); produce 想出;提出
  e.g. Some of the members have come up with suggestions of their own.
 

 

13. opportunity
n. 机会;良机
  e.g. You should go and see this film if you get the opportunity.

 

  

14. take advantage of
make use of 利用
  e.g. We took advantage of the fine weather to paint the fence.

 

15. afterward
ad. at a later time 后来;以后
  e.g. Not long afterward, he received the news that the man had died.
